Rare facts

panda

Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown)

Pandas are born looking like baby badgers — fur-less, pink, and blind. The iconic black and white colour comes later, after about three weeks.

dolphin

dolphins give themselves names

It has been proved by scientists that dolphins give themselves names. They develop their own individual whistles and they recognize theirs and other dolphins' names..

sea horse

Seahorses have no teeth and no stomach. Food passes through their digestive systems so quickly, they must eat almost constantly to stay alive.

Rabbit

Their teeth never stop growing

Like human fingernails, a rabbit's teeth will keep growing if given the chance. grow at a rate of up to 5 inches a year.

Kingfisher

They do blind fishing

They close their eyes as they dive into the water, so they are fishing blind! They bob their heads before diving to accurately judge the depth of the fish.
